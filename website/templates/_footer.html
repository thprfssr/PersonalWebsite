About Me

I'm a citizen of the world who loves mathematics and physics. I'm currently an undergrad working on my BS in Physics with Concentration in Engineering, a Minor in Astronomy, and a Minor in Mathematics. In my free time, I like to solve any physics and math problems which pique my interest.

As a hobby, I like to work on constructed languages, and to challenge myself to translate real-life text into my language. This is known as conlanging, and I consider it a form of art.

I'm also interested in programming, linguistics, astronomy, and a bunch of other nerdy topics.

In this website, I have all my personal projects, on all these different topics. Feel free to scour around wherever the links might take you!
